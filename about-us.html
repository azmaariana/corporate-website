Our Story

Stalo Group is a stationery company founded on January 2011 by Azma Ariana Binti Paizon, a 33 years old entrepreneur from Machang Kelantan. Stalo comes from the combination of two words which is Stationery and lovers. This stationery is not only dedicated to the stationery lovers but also to students, teachers and office worker because it sells various stationery products that consists of writing utensils, school and office equipment, paper products and also art supplies.On June 2013, Stalo Sdn Bhd launched their own products with the same brand name as their company name, Stalo.

Vision & Mission



Vision:
To become the world's leading brand of stationery business and become a premium brand.


Mission:
To provide best quality stationery products to students and offices at very affordable cost with the premium quality.
